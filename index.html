About

UDLab is an independent theoretical physics institute dedicated to the reformulation of fundamental laws through internally organized dynamics, spectral invariants, and functional unification. Our framework is grounded in algebraic consistency and operator theory, where physical observables emerge from the modal structure of compact self-adjoint operators — without invoking background geometry.

Highlights

  • Compact spectral formulations of gravitation and field theories
  • Operator-based unification of matter, energy, and entropy
  • Observable emergence from modal dominance and functional minimization
